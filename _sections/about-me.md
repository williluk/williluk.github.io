I'm Luke Williams, independent game developer. I've experimenting with every aspect of game development, from writing to art, audio to design.
    On the engineering side, I've done systems programming, shaders, and engine programming. I also have a background in research, studying how XR applications
    can be utilized in unique and accessible ways.

In more concrete terms, I worked on a series of interactive projects for Oregon State University's Extended Reality Laboratory, focusing on implementing highly usable
frontends and explore the applications of highly immersive VR. I've taught both game design and computer science courses at OSU's youth TAG program. I'm currently close
to releasing a Slay the Spire mod!

I'm an Oregon State University alumni, holding a B.S. in Applied Computer Science with a specialization in Simulation and Game Programming.
